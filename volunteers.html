How To Start

Join as a Volunteer. It's a very simple process.

1) Join our Discord server via this link.

2) Fill out the Google form with this link or go to the volunteer-application channel in the Discord.

3) You may need to create a new account or log into an existing one.

4) You will be given the pending volunteer role upon joining. You will be asked by our volunteer coordinator to receive some basic training. After this, you will be given the volunteer role and are able to sign up for sessions. More info about volunteer hours will be given once you're trained.

Additional Info

Here's some more things you need to know. Let's do this right here and right now.

Our volunteers are a wonderful team of native English speakers from across the U.S. and Canada. We provide service hours and chances to build leadership and communication skills through participating in our sessions. Volunteering with Bluebonnet Classroom is also an invaluable opportunity to interact with people from various countries and cultures around the world. Sessions are every weekend and last around forty-five minutes each. The topics and questions of the sessions are chosen by the host who is assisted by two additional volunteers to help the class run smoothly. Every week, the program administrator will post a sign up for session volunteers who can participate as either a host or an assistant. Though we encourage you to join whenever possible, you are not obligated to take part every week. Have any unique skills you can bring to the table? Be sure to mention it to our administrator when you sign up. We are a growing organization and always happy to have new people on board.
