Additional Info

Here's some more things you need to know. Let's do this right here and right now.

Currently, we offer sessions every Friday, Saturday, and Sunday at 8 pm, as well as Thursday and Saturday at 8 am U.S. Central Time that last around forty-five minutes each. A typical class is made up of three native English speakers with five learners, and our teachers are high school students from across the United States and Canada. One volunteer assumes the role of host to decide the topic and questions while the other two act as assistants to provide example answers and help the session run smoothly.

Bluebonnet Classroom is free for you to participate—all you need is a willingness to learn and speak. Before your class, make sure you have a stable internet connection and a device with the Zoom app installed.

While we would love to have you at each session, you are not obligated to attend classes every week. However, keep in mind that routine practice is key to mastering a second language and more participation will lead to greater benefits.
